Ingredients

For the Broth

  • 2 pounds beef bones
  • 1 onion, halved

  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 12 cups water
  • 1/2 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Salt to taste
