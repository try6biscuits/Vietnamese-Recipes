Buna Cha Recipe!

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • pork shoulder
  • pork belly
  • mint
  • basil
  • coriander
  • lettuce
  • bean sprouts,
  • carrots
  • daikon radish
  • rice vermicelli noodles

For Sauce

  • fish sauce
  • lime juice
  • sugar
  • garlic
  • chilies

Recipe

  • Marinate the pork: The pork is typically marinated in a mixture of
    fish sauce, sugar, garlic, shallots, lemongrass, and other spices.
  • Grill the pork: The marinated pork is then
    grilled over charcoal until cooked through.
  • Assemble the dish: Serve the grilled pork
    with the rice noodles, herbs, vegetables, and nuoc cham dipping sauce.
